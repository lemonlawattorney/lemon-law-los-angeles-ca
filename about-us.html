DIY Tips and Preventative Measures

While pursuing a Lemon Law claim is a legal process, there are steps you can take to protect yourself:

  • Maintain Complete Vehicle Records: Keep all repair orders, receipts, and communications from the dealer or manufacturer.
  • Report Issues Promptly: Document and report any defects as soon as they occur.
  • Consult a Lemon Law Expert: Early advice can save time and increase the likelihood of a favorable outcome.
